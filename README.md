##  To the Keri Foundation team

Welcome to The Linux Foundation as a new member of LF Decentralized Trust! We are excited to collaborate with you to build open source software, communities, standards, and specifications of blockchain, ledger, identity, cryptographic, and related technologies that are critical for the macro shift toward decentralized systems of distributed trust.

### The Keri Foundation's LF Decentralized Trust Team

You should have received an email requesting you to provide your name and contact information for additional team members in technical, marketing, and legal roles at your organization who will also be engaging with LF Decentralized Trust. Please let us know if you have any questions about the request. We look forward to collaborating with the broader team at The Keri Foundation.

### Welcome Aboard!

Our ecosystem team would like to learn more about your organization and its goals in joining LF Decentralized Trust. We will kick-start your membership with a personalized welcome call with your team to walk through the full onboarding process and discuss membership benefits details.

Schedule welcome call >> Sept 30 2025

### Vendor Directory

Companies visit our [Vendor Directory](https://www.lfdecentralizedtrust.org/use/vendor-directory), a member-only listing, to search for products and services built by LF Decentralized Trust members. The directory allows filtering by vendor offering types, geographies and industries as well as LF Decentralized Trust Projects. If you offer products or services built with our technologies, submit the request to join the growing commercial ecosystem.

Outreach Committee

The Outreach Committee is a great avenue to learn about our PR, event and marketing engagement opportunities, to get the latest updates on training, webinars, case studies and collateral resources, to hear from leaders in the LF Decentralized Trust community, and to collaborate with other marketers in the enterprise blockchain space. This group also has a monthly business development call on the 1st Tuesday of every month at 11am EST; the same call is also held at 2pm HKT 1st Wednesday of each month to accommodate participation from members located in the APAC region. Let us know who would be the best person on your team to receive communications about such opportunities.
